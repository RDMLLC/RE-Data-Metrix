Profit = (Total Investment)- (Rolled Costs) - (Lender Draw Fees) - (Closing Costs Sell) - (Commission Sell) 

We are concerned with three numbers here. 

1. ROI
The net profit/the total cost of the project, all in.
2. Cash-on-Cash Return
The net profit / The out of pocket
3. Annualized CoC ROI
The CoC Return * (12/project length)

Example:
$50,000 profit / $60,000 out of pocket = 83.3% cash on cash return
Annualized ROI is cash on cash ROI x (12/project length)
Example: 83.3% x (12/project length6) = 166.6%
Same example with a 4 month project is 249.9% annualized ROI
