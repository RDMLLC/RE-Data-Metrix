**Route:** `/max-offer-calculator`
**File:** `client/src/pages/MaxOfferCalculator.tsx`

- Rehab Budget (dollar)
- Maximum % of ARV (default 70%, adjustable)

### Outputs (live — recalculate on every input change, no Calculate button needed)

- Max Offer Price (Max Project Cost − Rehab Budget)

### UI notes

- Explainer callout box: "The 70% rule means you pay no more than 70% of the ARV minus rehab costs. This protects your profit margin on fix-and-flip deals."

## CALCULATOR 3 — DSCR Calculator
**Route:** `/dscr-calculator`
**File:** `client/src/pages/DscrCalculator.tsx`

### Inputs
**Property & Loan section**
- Purchase Price (dollar)
- Down Payment (%) — whole numbers only, no decimals
- Interest Rate (%) — 2 decimal places
- Loan Term — pill toggle: 15 / 20 / 30 years (default 30)
- Interest Only? Yes/No radio — if Yes, monthly payment = interest only (loan × rate/12)

**Income & Expenses section**
- Monthly Rent (dollar)
- Other Monthly Income (dollar, default 0) — label: "Other Income (laundry, parking, etc.)"
- Annual Property Tax (dollar)
- Annual Insurance (dollar)
- HOA Fee (monthly dollar, default 0)
- Vacancy Rate (%) — default 5%

### Outputs

**Primary result — large DSCR ratio, color-coded:**
- ≥ 1.25 → green — "Strong. Most lenders will approve."
- 1.00–1.24 → yellow/amber — "Marginal. Some lenders may approve."
- 0.75–0.99 → orange — "Negative cash flow. Limited lender options."
- < 0.75 → red — "Does not qualify for most DSCR programs."

**Secondary metrics (shown in a grid below the primary result):**
- Loan Amount
- Monthly P&I Payment (or Interest Only payment)
- Monthly PITIA (P&I + taxes/12 + insurance/12 + HOA)
- Monthly Cash Flow (monthly rent + other income − PITIA)
- Annual Cash Flow
- NOI (gross rent × (1 − vacancy%) − annual tax − annual insurance − HOA×12)
- Cap Rate (NOI / purchase price × 100)

**"What would it take to hit 1.25 DSCR?" section:**
- Required Monthly Rent to reach 1.25 DSCR (PITIA × 1.25)
- Required Down Payment % to reach 1.25 DSCR (solve for down payment where new PITIA × 1.25 = current rent)
- Show both as: current value → required value, with the gap highlighted

### Formulas
loanAmount = purchasePrice × (1 − downPayment/100)
monthlyPI = interestOnly
? loanAmount × (rate/12)
: loanAmount × (r × (1+r)^n) / ((1+r)^n − 1)   where r = rate/1200, n = term×12
monthlyPITIA = monthlyPI + annualTax/12 + annualInsurance/12 + hoaMonthly
effectiveMonthlyIncome = (monthlyRent + otherMonthlyIncome) × (1 − vacancyRate/100)
DSCR = (monthlyRent + otherMonthlyIncome) / monthlyPITIA  // gross rent, not NOI — matches lender underwriting
annualNOI = (monthlyRent + otherMonthlyIncome) × 12 × (1 − vacancyRate/100) − annualTax − annualInsurance − hoaMonthly×12
capRate = annualNOI / purchasePrice × 100
monthlyCashFlow = effectiveMonthlyIncome − monthlyPITIA

### UI notes
- Two-column layout: Property & Loan left, Income & Expenses right — matching Rental Property Calculator
- Calculate button (not live recalc) — same pattern as Rental Property Calculator
- Results section appears below after Calculate clicked
- DSCR ratio displayed very large (text-5xl or larger), color matches threshold
- "What would it take" section uses a two-column comparison: "Current" vs "Required for 1.25"
- Download PDF: branded RE Data Metrix header, inputs summary, all outputs including "what would it take" section
