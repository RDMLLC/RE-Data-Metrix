One of the most underused features for new members is the deal tracking lifecycle. Here is the full picture of how it works:

From Analysis to Sold

  • 1 Analyze — Run any property through the Deal Analysis Wizard. The platform calculates projected ROI, profit, holding costs, and compares loan options automatically.
  • 2 Save — Save the analysis to your portal. It starts in "Analyzing" status so you can come back to it anytime.
  • 3 Track — Move the deal through statuses as it progresses: Under Contract, Purchased, and finally Sold.
  • 4 Measure — When you mark a deal as Sold, enter your actuals. The platform compares your real ROI and profit against your original projections.

Over time, this builds a record of your investing performance — which deals hit their targets, which ones did not, and why.
