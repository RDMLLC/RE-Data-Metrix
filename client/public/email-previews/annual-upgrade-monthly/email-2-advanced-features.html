You already know the core features well. This week, we want to highlight a few things that monthly members often overlook — but that annual members tend to use consistently once they discover them.

State-Specific Calculators

Standard deal calculators use national averages for closing costs and transfer taxes. State-specific calculators use your actual state's rates — which means more accurate profit projections, especially in high-tax states.

Try it in your next analysis →

PDF & CSV Report Exports

Any saved deal can be exported as a professional PDF to share with partners or lenders, or as a CSV for your own tracking spreadsheets. If you are presenting deals to other investors or building a portfolio, this feature alone saves significant time.

Export a saved deal →

Saved Deals as a Pipeline

Rather than running each analysis in isolation, try saving every property you evaluate — even ones you pass on. Over time, your saved deals become a searchable record of your market activity and decision-making history.

View your pipeline →

Pro tip: When you save a lender alongside a saved deal, your deal file includes both the analysis and the financing source — making it easy to pick back up on a deal months later without losing context.
