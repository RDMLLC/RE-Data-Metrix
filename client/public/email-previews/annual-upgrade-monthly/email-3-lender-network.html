Making the Most of the Lender Network

Hi ${Contact.First Name},

You have had access to the lender network as a monthly member. Now that you are on annual, it is worth taking a closer look at how to get the most out of it — particularly for deal types you may not have explored yet.

Matching Lenders to Deal Types

Fix-and-Flip

Filter for hard money or bridge lenders in your state. Look for those with fast closing timelines (10–14 days) and flexible rehab draw schedules to keep your project moving.

Buy-and-Hold / BRRRR

Filter for DSCR lenders. These qualify you on the property's rental income rather than your personal income — making it easier to scale a rental portfolio without hitting conventional loan limits.

Wholesale / Double Close

Filter for transactional lenders. Same-day funding with no seasoning requirements means your double close can move on your buyer's timeline, not your lender's.

Long-Term Holds

Filter for conventional lenders if you qualify on personal financials, or DSCR lenders if you prefer to keep personal and investment income separate.

Browse the Lender Network

Build a Lender Shortlist

As you browse, save lenders that match your deal types to your portal. Over time you will build a go-to list for each strategy — so when a deal is ready, you know exactly who to call.

Tip: You can save a lender directly to a specific saved deal in your portal, keeping your financing source attached to the property it is for.
