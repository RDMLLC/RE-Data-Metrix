How Saved Deals Work

1
Run a deal analysis

Walk any property through the Deal Analysis Wizard to generate your projected ROI, holding costs, and profit estimate.

2
Save it to your portal

Hit save and the deal is stored in your personal portal. Name it, tag it, and come back to it anytime.

3
Export when you need it

Download any saved deal as a professional PDF or CSV to share with partners, lenders, or for your own records.

4
Track your lenders alongside your deals

Save preferred lenders from the network to your portal so your financing options are always alongside your deal pipeline.
