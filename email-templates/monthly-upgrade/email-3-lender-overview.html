Two weeks into your membership — by now you have probably run a few deal analyses. The natural next step is making sure you have the right financing lined up. That is where the lender network comes in.

With your paid membership, you have unlimited access to compare lenders side by side. Here is a breakdown of what is available:

Hard Money / Bridge Lenders

Short-term financing with fast closings. Asset-based underwriting means approval is based on the deal, not your personal financials.

Best for: Fix-and-flip projects, quick acquisitions

DSCR (Rental) Lenders

Long-term loans qualified on rental income rather than personal income. No tax returns or W-2s required.

Best for: Buy-and-hold portfolio building

Transactional Lenders

Same-day funding for wholesale double closes. No seasoning requirements, minimal documentation.

Best for: Wholesale investors doing double closes

Conventional Lenders

Traditional mortgage products for investors who prefer standard loan terms and qualify on personal financials.

Best for: Long-term holds with strong personal credit

You can filter lenders by state, loan type, closing speed, and specific criteria like "works with new investors" or "100% funding available." Save your preferred lenders to your portal for quick access on future deals.
